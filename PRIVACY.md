This extension does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data.

All actions are performed locally in the browser and communicate only with the user's own Huawei B311‑221 router. No information is sent to external servers or third parties.

The extension does not use cookies, analytics, tracking scripts or any form of user profiling.

If you have questions about this policy, you can contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store support page.
